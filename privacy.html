Forma Privacy Policy

This page describes how Forma handles your information when you use the app.

Last updated: October 2024

How we handle your data

We use Firebase Authentication to let you log in with your Google account. We do not store, collect, or share any personal data on our own servers. All authentication is handled securely by Firebase, a service provided by Google.

What we do

  • Authenticate you with your Google account via Firebase.
  • Rely on Firebase’s secure infrastructure to manage sign-in and session handling.
  • Do not create our own user profiles, databases, or behavioral tracking.

Your choices

  • You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account’s security settings.
  • You can stop using the app at any time; we do not retain any personal data.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, please contact us at hello@forma.house.
